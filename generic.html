About Me

I'm sure if you are on this page you are trying to find out more about who I am. Other than my technical background, I will tell you about myself here and show that I am not just a code monkey :D

Front End Developer with real world experience using multiple javascript frameworks plus a background in strength & conditioning coaching.

As a kid I was in love with science and technology and that enthusiasm fell off when I started studying computer science in college. After a year hiatus from higher education I decided to give coding another shot.

My experience in strength programming lent itself to immersion in Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, React and Redux. Meanwhile my experience in building client relationships evokes relentless drive to problem solve.

I have since applied these skills to my role as a frontend developer at Tango Analytics and now hope to bring my skills to another company to grow and learn more!

In my free time I like to go to the gym, I am a competitive powerlifter and love training for that. I also spend my time working on my powerlifting coaching business, I write programs and communicate with my athletes on a daily/weekly basis. Other than that I spend my time learning more and more about code. I love learning and that really became a big part of me while I was working through the FlatIron School, and I love how I am still learning today at Tango Analytics!

What's next for me? Currently as a project for my portfolio, I am finishing up a web app tiktok clone using ReactJS. In addition, I plan on moving back to New York City, which is why I am looking for roles that are remote or are in New York. I would also love to add more JS frameworks to my toolkit and I plan on experimenting more with nodeJS and deepening my skills in VueJS, ReactJS and other frameworks that work well with those two. Most importantly, I am looking to learn more and level up as an engineer and would love to become a senior engineer in a few years!
