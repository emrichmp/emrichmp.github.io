About Me

I'm sure if you are on this page you are trying to find out more about who I am. Other than my technical background, I will tell you about myself here and show that I am not just a code monkey :D

Full stack web developer with a hunger for problem solving. A graduate of the FlatIron School with a passion for strength and conditioning coaching. Growing up I was in love with science and technology as a kid and that enthusiasm fell off when I started studying computer science in college. After a year hiatus from higher education I decided to give coding another shot. My experience in strength programming lent itself to immersion in Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, React and Redux. Meanwhile my experience in building client relationships evokes relentless drive to problem solve.

In my free time I like to go to the gym, I am a competitive powerlifter and love training for that. I also spend my time working on my powerlifting coaching business, I write programs and communicate with my athletes on a daily/weekly basis. Other than that I spend my time learning more and more about code. I love learning and that really became a big part of me while I was working through the FlatIron School, and I love how I am still learning today at Tango Analytics! What's next for me? I plan to learn how to use other popular JS libraries such as node.js and would like to learn how to use Python3. Additionally, I would love to see if I'd like to be in data science and I plan on taking the Google course on it. Other than that I spend my time making youtube videos and playing with my dog Kilo! I love to explore and walk around the city too!
