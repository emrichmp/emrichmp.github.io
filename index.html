About Me

My name is Emrich-Michael Sarte Perrier and I am a half-filipino Software Engineer, Powerlifter and Coach originally from Queens, New York! For the past 2 years I have been living in Los Angeles chasing my goals as a developer and a powerlifter!

Developer Background

I started my dev journey as a frontend development UI Intern at Tango Analytics, which led to me being promoted to Jr. Frontend Developer until Tango and I parted ways in March '23. In December '22 I started building my Upwork account, which lead to me landing my first project my first month. After spending more time building my account and sending numerous proposals I landed 4 projects in March '23 and then my first long term "higher rate" project in May '23 building an app for OFTV. Since then I have spent my time working as an LGTV and Vue Developer for Goyk Productions working on the OnlyFans extension OFTV. In addition, I have started builing out Ourmada (a web3 tool) using both frontend and backend technologies which has given me so much room to grow an excel as a developer. Given this journey, I am super proud of my growth as a developer; Starting as an intern teaching myself TypeScript and 2 versions of both Vue & Quasar to builing full scale high traffic frontend & fullstack applications has been quite the journey.

What do I do Outside of Work?

I'm glad you asked! Let's break it down...

I spend a lot of my time as a Powerlifter and a Powerlifting Coach! Having as many as 13 clients, I have spent a lot of time guiding others through training and through competitions. I provide meet day coaching for my athletes and connect with them once a week, just like my Upwork clients! For my own goals, I track my macros to best eat performance and train 4-5 times a week. Currently my all time PR numbers are 452 lbs Squat, 330 lbs Bench and 525 lbs Deadlift. I hope to hit 500/363/585 by the end of 2024!

I love to cook food! Growing up in a Filipino house-hold, I loved watching my mom cook and picked up cooking skills from watching her! Since Late 2019 I have been a home cook and have experimented with all types of cuisine! I've found it super fun to utilize cooking techniques I've learned online to enhance the flavor of more "macro-friendly" foods! In addition, I love cooking for friends/family and just successfully cooked/hosted my 3rd friendsgiving! I love cooking French, Italian, Asian and Mexican cuisine!

I love to stay active and I am a huge walker! I also have played tennis since I was 7 years old and you can catch me tearing it up at Echo Park at least once a week! I love biking around as well! Biking around both LA and NYC has been a great past time for me and I have done the 5 borough bike tour for 5 seperate years! In addition, I love finding ways to express myself artistically!
