Background

Emrich took his first coding course in his junior year of highschool. That sparked a passion to pursue Computer Science at Syracuse University and Computer Systems at City Tech. With a declining passion for STEM Emrich took a step away from coding but found that passion again during the pandemic with the FlatIron School. Besides spending countless hours learning Full Stack Web Development, Emrich has also spent his time building his powerlifting coaching business, where he has developed a skill to build strong client relationships with people. He loves to work and help people. Besides engineering Emrich loves to compete in powerlifting, play with his dog Kilo and explore the city. New York City born and raised, Emrich has just relocated to Los Angeles California and is now looking to find work in the SE in the LA area or work remotely.

June, 2021

myFoodJournal - Nutrition Tracker!

This react.js based web app brings all your tracking needs! myFoodJournal lets a user to add food to their meals in their diary. They also may add their goals and macros so they can hit those goals and track along with it. If a user doesn't know where to start they can also use the included TDEE calculator to find an estimated caloric intake and a recommended macro breakdown!
